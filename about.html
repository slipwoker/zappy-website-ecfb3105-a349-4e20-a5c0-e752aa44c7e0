Maria Saldivar

Founder & Lead Mediator

With over fifteen years of experience in family law mediation, Maria Saldivar has dedicated her career to helping families navigate some of life's most challenging transitions. A former family law attorney, she witnessed firsthand how adversarial litigation can deepen conflict and prolong emotional distress — especially when children are involved. That realization led her to establish Saldivar Family Law Mediation, a practice built on the belief that families deserve a better path forward.

Maria's approach is grounded in neutrality, empathy, and a deep respect for each family's unique dynamics. She creates a structured yet compassionate environment where all parties feel heard, guiding them toward mutually acceptable solutions for divorce, child custody, and asset division. Her goal is not simply to reach an agreement, but to help families build a foundation for healthier communication long after the mediation concludes.

A Commitment to Respectful Resolution

Every mediation session is guided by three core principles: preserving dignity, prioritizing the well-being of children, and empowering families to shape their own futures. Maria believes that even in the midst of conflict, there is an opportunity for clarity, healing, and a respectful transition to the next chapter.

Our Philosophy

We believe that even the most difficult family transitions deserve a process rooted in respect, clarity, and dignity. Our approach to family law mediation is built on a foundation of core values that guide every conversation we facilitate.

Neutrality

We maintain a balanced, impartial presence in every session. As a family law mediation attorney, our role is not to take sides but to create a structured space where both parties can be heard equally, fostering an environment where fair outcomes can emerge naturally.

Dignity

Every individual deserves to navigate family transitions with their self-respect intact. We uphold a standard of courtesy and compassion that protects the emotional well-being of everyone involved, ensuring that the mediation process never adds unnecessary harm to an already challenging situation.

Clarity & Child-Centered Focus

We cut through legal complexity with plain language and transparent guidance. Above all, we keep the best interests of children at the heart of every decision, helping parents craft parenting plans and agreements that prioritize stability, security, and long-term well-being for their families.
